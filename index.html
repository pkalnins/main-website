    Paul Kalnins, ND, MS

    Bridging traditional healing and modern science through systems and network-based medicine

    Welcome

    I’m Paul Kalnins, ND, MS — a naturopathic physician, biomedical data scientist, and independent researcher based in Portland, Oregon. My work explores how systems science and network physiology can bridge traditional healing frameworks with modern biomedical data.

    I develop computational and clinical models that examine health as an interconnected, multi-organ system — integrating electronic health record analytics, physiological networks, and whole-person clinical care. Through research, mentorship, and writing, I focus on building practical frameworks for understanding resilience, chronic disease, and recovery across biological scales.

    Core Activities

    Telehealth

    Telemedicine consults for endocrine, nervous, immune, and organ system health using nutritional, herbal, and integrative strategies.

    Teaching

    One-on-one mentorship for students and practitioners in naturopathic and herbal medicine, with a focus on systems-based clinical reasoning.

    Research

    Systems- and network-based biomedical research integrating EHR data, wearable analytics, and physiological modeling to study resilience, chronic disease, and whole-person health.
